 Dambulla

    Dabulla city is located at a distance of 147 km from Colombo. It is located in Matale district and can be considered as the second largest city after Matale. Sri Lanka's grant vegetable distribution is located in Madhyastan and there are many educationally unique sights to see here for tourists. Among them, the preserved cave temple, Sigiriya, Rangiri Dabulu playground, the world's largest rose quartz waterfall, the ironwood forest and the swimming pool can be noted. Located 3km away from the cave temples, the Ibbankattuwa Prehistoric Burial Ground is a great testimony to the indigenous civilization of Sri Lanka before the arrival of Indians.

 Kandy

    Kandy is named as an administrative city in Sri Lanka. Sri Dalada maligawa is considered to be the grantee here. In 1988, UNESCO named it as a World Heritage Site.

    city tour:

    After taking breakfast from your hotel, we are going to go for city tour. Both sides of the road have very beautiful surroundings and consist of gardens. You can see nearly 300 species of orchids in these gardens. Every year nearly 1.2 million people come to see the wonder of these 147-acre gardens. And Sri Lanka's gems Museum is also located in this city. After watching all this, you can end your day and spend the night at your hotel after attending a special traditional and cultural dance show in the evening.

 Galle

    Enjoy a delicious breakfast at your hotel. Today you will visit Galle.

    city tour:

    During your city tour you will visit the Galle Dutch Fort which is among the most popular sites in Galle where it attracts those who value archaeological reserves and heritage. It is known as the oldest fort in Asia, named as a world heritage by UNESCO. Inside the fort, travelers can find other attractions such as the Dutch Museum, which contains paintings, pictures and furniture of the Dutch Reformed Church. The fort is built on small peninsula that can be easily accessed from the Galle town. It is the best place to visit during an evening as the picturesque scenery of the sunset can be witnessed at its best. The trilingual inscription is in the National Museum in Colombo and a copy can be found in the Maritime Museum in Galle.

  Bentota

    Bentota is a beautiful beach town in Galle district. Located 65 km south of Colombo, this city is a very suitable center for beach activities. It is a very safe beach for anyone big or small to relax and take a bath.you can get your hands at snorkeling, sailing, windsurfing, kavaking, and deep sea fishing.

 Colombo

    Colombo can be called the largest and only commercial city in Sri Lanka. Trains operate from Colombo Fort to travel to any station in Sri Lanka. Located close to the west coast, this city of Colombo is a place where many business activities take place in the country. The city is very busy with many entertainment venues like shopping malls, cinemas, etc.
