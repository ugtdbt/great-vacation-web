  About Us

Take a break from your monotonous lifestyle and make wonderful memories with an exciting holiday package from great-vacations, which is a tourist board-approved travel agency with experience of over 20 years.

As one of the leading travel agency in Sri Lanka, we have organized cultural tours with the essence of Sri Lanka’s diverse cultural experience, honeymoon tours for loving couples with serene and romantic getaways, private retreat holidays in secluded boutique hotels, beach tours for tranquil holidays, destination weddings, and celebration planning and arrangement, and adventurous programs for thrill-seekers.

We have packages not only for popular destinations but also for the destinations that are still out of the radar.

We not only provide a wide range of holiday packages to destinations, but also help you to plan your trip, organising itineraries to suit your excursion interests, arranging accommodation and your transport requirements.

You can enjoy your vacation by selecting from our extensive range of curated packages or building your own packages. Whether you are travelling for your honeymoon, a family getaway, a solo excursion or a vacation with your friends, you can customize your itinerary according to your budget and preferences.

We provide qualified and well experienced National Tourist Guides and Chauffeurs Tourist Guides who speak English, French, Spanish, German, Arab and Hindi fluently. They cover everything from leading tourist attractions and restaurants to hotels and how to get to a certain spot. You can refer to our guides before you visit a place to get a clear idea of what to expect and any facts you should know before visiting.
